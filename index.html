Minas Gerais

Minas Gerais is an enchanting state, known for its breathtaking mountainous landscapes, waterfalls, and vast horizons that captivate any visitor. Its historical richness dates back to the colonial period, marked by the gold rush, which left behind a legacy of historic towns like Ouro Preto, Mariana, and Tiradentes, filled with baroque architecture and cultural traditions. Moreover, Minas stands out for its cultural diversity, unique cuisine, and the warmth of its people, making it a true treasure for Brazil.
